# ScreenShorganiser 📸

> A screenshot triage and archiving tool. Manual control, smart assistance.

You know the feeling. Hundreds — maybe thousands — of screenshots sitting in a folder. Game moments, maths workings, random captures, actual memories. All of them named `Screenshot 2026-04-17 141259`. All of them completely unsorted.

ScreenShorganiser is being built to fix that.

---

## The idea

Not an AI that guesses what your screenshots are. Not an automated sorter that gets it wrong. A fast, powerful tool that puts *you* in control — and makes the decisions effortless.

Browse by date. Spot patterns at a glance. Select a whole day in one click. Delete, archive, zip, or push to the cloud. Your screenshots, your call.

Inspired by WizTree's philosophy of making overwhelming data instantly scannable and actionable.

---

## Coming in v0.1

- Load a folder of screenshots
- View them grouped by date with thumbnails
- Select individual files or entire days at once
- Delete the selection

## Planned features

- Date range search
- Storage analytics — see which days are eating your disk
- Bulk rename with custom naming conventions
- Export to zip
- Google Drive upload
- Exact duplicate detection
- Slideshow / review mode — keyboard-driven triage
- Starring — protect your best shots from bulk actions
- Recycle bin safety net
- Timeline heatmap — visualise your screenshot history
- Multi-source folder support
